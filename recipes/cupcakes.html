Cupcakes

Description

Cupcakes are an old time classic! This is such a common and known recipe, that i dont even know if i should tell you how to make it!

Nevertheless, here is how you can make some delicious cupcakes for family or friends:

ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 cup grated monterey jack cheese
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes , prepared food coloring

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Line 12 muffin tin cups with foil bake cups.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together all of the meat loaf ingredients until well combined. Divide the mixture evenly among the lined cups (the liners should be about three quarters full).
  4. Place the filled muffin tins on a cookie sheet and bake the cupcakes for about 15 minutes or until cooked through.
  5. Divide the mashed potatoes among three small bowls and stir a few drops of food coloring into each batch to create blue, yellow, and pink pastel frostings.
  6. Spread a generous dollop on each cupcake.

    7. And there you have it folks! Now you can enjoy these cupcakes as much as my family enjoyed them!
