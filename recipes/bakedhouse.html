Baked House

Description

We have all been there, we want to eat something, but there is nothing in the fridge! I know that i have! Today i present you with the easiest recipe of them all:

Baked House! Here is how to make it:

Ingridients

  • 1 Oven
  • 5 liters of gasoline(additional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 1000 degrees
  2. Pour the gasoline over the areas you want to be baked
  3. IMPORTANT make sure that the gasoline is connected to the oven
  4. And now wait for about 40-50 min

    5. No need for thanking me! This will save a lot of hungry nights!
